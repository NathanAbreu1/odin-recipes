Parmegian Beef

The Parmegian beef is a recipe used in different parts of the world,it varies depending on the region you are,likely to have different ingredients and sauces!

The ingredients are very simple,it'll mostly need an ounce of meat,some tomatos and a little tomato sauce

ingredients

  • Piece of meat
  • 4 tomatos
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. fry the meat
  2. cut the tomatos
  3. add the sauce on the cooked meat

Enjoy your meal ;)
