Rustic Fries

rustic Fries is a very easy recipe that you can cook on your home,with basic ingredient,they are fantastic to follow the parmegian beef

The ingredients besides being pretty simple,are also pretty cheap,so enjoy your meal

    ingredients

    • 3 potatos
    • oregano sauce
    • any bitter sauce
    • salt

    Steeps

    1. First of all,cut the potatos in wave forms
    2. After that,tost all of the sauce you got in the potatos
    3. Then,throw the potatos at the air frier
